Beginners Chen Style Taichi

Thursdays 7:30pm - 8:30pm, starting Oct. 10, 2013
Instructor: Master Yijiao Hong, assisted by Sifu Blake Emery

Taught by Master Yijiao Hong, and assisted by Sifu Blake Emery. Master Hong (an official disciple of Grandmaster Chen Zhenglei) will give in-depth, step-by-step instruction on authentic Chen Taichi form movements, and Qigong warm-ups from Traditional Chinese Medicine, using the latest in physical learning science. You will also learn how Taichi originated from ancient Chinese philosophy and Chinese medicine. This class will emphasize the skills of self-centering, body relaxation, and slow breathing as you practice form movements. You can expect increased flexibility, leg strength, coordination, and balance, as well as learning to be relaxed in an energetic way.

Tuition: $160, plus $50 for registration and uniforms.

Quarter Schedule

CWTA operates on a quarterly schedule, each quarter lasting 10 weeks.

For 2013, the quarter schedule is:

  • 1st quarter: January 7 - March 17
  • 2nd quarter: April 8 - June 16
  • 3rd quarter: July 15 - September 22
  • 4th quarter: October 7 - December 15

Registration & Payment

If you are new, submit a registration form to register now! Add $50 for uniform and registration fee.

Payment is due before the end of the previous quarter. Payments received after the due date are subject to a $20 late fee.

Please make checks payable to CWTA LLC.

Please note: no refund / mininum one quarter commitment

Makeup Policy

Sometimes students will miss one or more classes due to an out of town trip, sickness etc. Students can make up classes by attending any similar class that occurs on another day. Please check with instructor to see what classes are available to use as make up classes. Also, instructors are available 10 minutes Before and after class to work with you on the new movements when you return to class.
 

Private Lessons

For private Yang Style Taichi Lessons, contact Sifu James Guidon at 206-527-0144 ($50/hour/person)

James is a graduate of the University of Washington with a Bachelors of Fine Arts Degree in Painting. He has been learning and practicing Internal Martial Arts since 1979. He has been teaching Yang Style Taichi since 1993; twelve years with the Chinese Wushu and Taichi Academy. James is currently a student of Master Yijiao Hong and has been since 1997. James completed teacher training program in 2000 with Master Hong and is certified Yang Style Taichi instructor with over 20 years experience working with the Art of taichi to bring body and mind in Harmony and to experience the benefit of this practice - Health and Tranquility.
