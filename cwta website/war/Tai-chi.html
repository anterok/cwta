Tai Chi Chuan is one of China's most venerated schools of Kungfu. Literally translated as 'grand ultimate fist', this ancient self-defense discipline is widely practiced today by both men and women, young and old, as a method of self-cultivation.

Descending from a profound cosmological concept, Tai Chi is symbolized by the unification of duality—yin and yang. Its slow, introspective methods are deeply meditative, highly therapeutic, and conceal deadly combat techniques.
